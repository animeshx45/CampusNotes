# **App Name**: NIT StudySync

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure user registration and login functionality, allowing students to access and contribute materials.
- Material Upload & Management: Users can upload various study materials (notes, assignments, PDFs) to relevant sections with appropriate metadata.
- Categorized Browsing: Browse study materials organized clearly by academic branch (Electrical, IT, CS, Mechanical, Chemical, Civil) and then by semester.
- Material Search & Filtering: Robust search functionality to find specific notes or study materials, with filters for semester, branch, and material type.
- NIT Srinagar Branding: Display of the official NIT Srinagar logo and relevant institutional links.
- AI Study Aid Tool: An AI tool that can generate concise summaries or potential exam questions from uploaded study materials, assisting students in quick revision.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, deep ocean blue (#1e4a77), conveying trust and intellectual depth, drawing inspiration from academic tradition and clarity.
- Background color: A very subtle, cool light grey-blue (#e9edf2), providing a clean, non-distracting canvas for content and enhancing readability.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear indigo (#5c2cc5), offering a compelling contrast for calls to action and highlighting important elements.
- Headlines and section titles will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, crisp, and organized aesthetic. Body text for material descriptions and detailed information will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent readability across all devices.
- Minimalist and modern line icons will be used to represent actions (upload, search, filter) and categories (branches, semesters), maintaining a clean UI.
- A responsive, grid-based layout ensuring optimal viewing and interaction on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones, with clear segregation of sections for branches and semesters.
- Subtle and purposeful animations for page transitions, material loading, and interactive elements, providing a smooth and engaging user experience without being distracting.
